How to Play

  • 1
    Paste an image URL into the text box and click 'Start Game'
  • 2
    ChatGPT will generate a description for you to guess
  • 3
    For each word, you'll see its first and last letter, plus all occurrences of those letters within the word
  • 4
    Fill in all letters and press Enter on your keyboard to check your guess
  • 5
    The letters will be colored to show your progress:
    Green: Correct letter in correct position
    Yellow: Correct letter in wrong position
